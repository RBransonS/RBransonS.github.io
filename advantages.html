Height Advantages

Some advantages for someone my height include the following.

  • Having an authoritative appearance
  • Being able to reach things on high shelves
  • Helping in some sports

    • The authoritative appearance can help when applying for authority positions within jobs, clubs, or organizations. The reaching high things off the shelves allows you to, well reach things most people cannot. The sports one is an obvious yet deceiving subject. While it is beneficial for sports such as basketball or American football, it is equally problematic for other sports which I will list on the Height Disadvantages page.
