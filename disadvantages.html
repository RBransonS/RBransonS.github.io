Height Disadvantages

Height disadvantages include, but are not limited to, the following.

  • Increased risk of physical injuries
  • Reduced lifespan
  • Things don’t come in my size
      • Doorways
      • Cars
      • Clothes
      • Beds
  • Sports

    • Being this tall, I have personally had 3 back surgeries in the span of 5 years. It also is problematic on the joints and the head (more on this momentarily). Another significant issue is having a greatly reduced lifespan on average due to the increased strain the heart must undergo to pump blood throughout the whole body.

    Now back onto the head injuries… Doorways. The standard issue doorways are 6 foot 8 inches. This is problematic for those of us taller than that for obvious reasons. Take this picture of me standing in a doorway in my house for instance. Comical yet painful.

    The last thing I want to mention is sports. These are harder for taller individuals. These sports include tennis, CrossFit, baseball and soccer just to name a few.
